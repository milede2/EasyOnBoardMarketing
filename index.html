Who We Are
Structure without complexity

One portal. Every workflow.

Easy Onboard brings HR administration, onboarding checklists, IT asset tracking, and internal documentation into a single, self-hosted Django application your team can rely on.

Onboarding complete Sarah M. — just now
Easy Onboard was born out of frustration with the patchwork of tools teams cobble together for new hires — a shared drive here, a task list there, and a dozen emails in between. We built a purpose-designed portal so everything lives in one place.
Deployed inside your own infrastructure — your data stays yours
Built with Django — proven, maintainable, and easy to extend
Designed for SMBs who want structure without enterprise complexity
HR, IT, and employees all work from the same source of truth
What We Offer
Everything your team needs,
nothing it doesn't

Six focused modules that cover the full onboarding lifecycle — from the moment an offer is signed to the day your new hire is fully productive.

HR Dashboard

Manage employees, departments, and workflows from a single command centre. See headcount, track status, and stay on top of every detail.

Employee Onboarding

Assign tailored workflows with trackable steps employees can check off themselves. Everyone knows exactly what's expected and when.

IT Asset Tracking

Full hardware inventory with status tracking and employee assignment. Know exactly who has what, and what's available for the next hire.

Permission Management

Employees request system access, HR reviews and approves. A clean audit trail for every permission granted or denied.

Documentation Hub

Centralised company policies, handbooks, and guides. New hires find answers on their own instead of chasing down colleagues.

Reports & Analytics

Onboarding progress, headcount trends, bottleneck identification, and IT overview — the numbers you need to make informed decisions.
