Help Center
Getting started

Easy Onboard is designed to simplify the employee onboarding process from start to finish. Once a new hire is added to the system, a structured onboarding workflow is automatically assigned, guiding both the employee and internal teams through each required step. HR teams can manage employee records, assign onboarding tasks, and monitor progress through a central dashboard. IT teams can track asset allocation and handle access requests, while employees can complete tasks, request permissions, and access documentation — all in one place. For additional support, users can contact our team directly or refer to internal documentation provided within the platform.

Privacy
Your data stays yours

Easy Onboard is built with privacy as a core principle. All employee and organisational data is stored securely within your own infrastructure, ensuring full control over sensitive information. We do not sell, share, or use your data for any third-party purposes. Access to data is strictly controlled through role-based permissions, ensuring that only authorised users can view or modify information. The platform is designed to align with GDPR principles, including data minimisation, purpose limitation, and secure handling of personal data.

Security
Built with security in mind

Security is embedded throughout Easy Onboard's architecture. The platform uses secure authentication mechanisms and role-based access control to ensure that users only access what they are authorised to see. All actions within the system are logged, providing a clear audit trail for onboarding activities, permissions, and changes. This ensures accountability and transparency across HR and IT workflows. Regular updates and best practices in Django development are followed to maintain a secure and reliable system.

Usage & responsibilities

Easy Onboard is provided as a platform to support internal employee onboarding processes. By using the system, organisations are responsible for ensuring that the data entered is accurate, lawful, and compliant with applicable regulations. Users must not misuse the platform, attempt to gain unauthorised access, or disrupt system functionality. While we aim to provide a reliable and secure service, Easy Onboard is offered “as is” without guarantees of uninterrupted availability. Ongoing improvements and updates may be made to enhance performance and functionality.
